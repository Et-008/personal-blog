I'm a self thaught software engineer. Just a normal dood with mediocre expectations on life.

#### My alter ego

Every day i go to work at 10 in the morning, login to my laptop and start coding. It was an interesting job with high expectations and short deadlines. Designers were responsible for deciding what to be done, Marketers were responsible for deciding when to be done, trying and meeting those design expectations and deadlines were my job. Going to work getting my job done and returning home just to sleep, was my routine and i was good at it, until that one day.


#### The incident

My work life was going busy and peaceful. It was the month of february, some new employees joined and there she was(the one - atleast i thought so).
The perfect way to describe her, "waaayyyy out of my league".
Entered the office crossed my table, still remember the way she walked past me.
I was happy to go to work just to see her, she knew about it but couldn't care less.
Everything was going perfect as it should be - I was looking for her in every girl i see, while she ignored my existence.


#### With great power, comes great pain

Soon a delightful thing happened, some of her friends found out. They were referencing me to tease her and i was on a 52 week high.
One day i was sitting at a viewing distance from her and her group. So this guy friend of hers started teasing her and the group joined him. Immediately he stops them by saying these exact words "Hey guys enough enough, stop it. She's gonna cry again".

"Again? did he say again?" 🤯. She cried? because of me?
The worst has happened, to get irritated is something, but to **cry**. 

That is when i realised i have this super power - I can make a girl cry, just by looking at her.
I have this super power, which is of no use to me or anyone else but none the less i'm still a super hero😎.
